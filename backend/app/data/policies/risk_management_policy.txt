TITLE: Risk Management Policy

SECTION: Risk Identification
Project teams must identify potential risks at the beginning of the project.
Risks may include technical risks, schedule risks, or resource risks.

SECTION: Risk Assessment
Each risk should be evaluated based on:
- Probability of occurrence
- Potential impact on the project

Risks should be categorized as low, medium, or high.

SECTION: Risk Mitigation
For each high or medium risk, mitigation strategies must be defined.
Mitigation may include additional testing, resource allocation, or schedule adjustments.

SECTION: Risk Monitoring
Risks must be reviewed periodically during project meetings.
New risks should be added to the risk register when identified.
