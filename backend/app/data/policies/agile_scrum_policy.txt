TITLE: Agile / Scrum Policy

SECTION: Sprint Planning
Sprint Planning is conducted at the beginning of each sprint.
The development team selects backlog items based on priority and team capacity.
The output of Sprint Planning is a Sprint Backlog.

SECTION: Daily Scrum
Daily Scrum is a short meeting held every working day.
The meeting should be time-boxed to 15 minutes.
Each team member answers three questions:
1. What did I complete yesterday?
2. What will I work on today?
3. What obstacles are blocking my progress?

SECTION: Sprint Review
Sprint Review is conducted at the end of each sprint.
The team demonstrates completed features to stakeholders.
Stakeholders provide feedback which may lead to backlog updates.

SECTION: Definition of Done
A backlog item is considered done when:
- Code is implemented
- Code review is completed
- Unit tests pass
- Documentation is updated
