TITLE: Change Management Policy

SECTION: Change Request
Any modification to project scope, requirements, or timeline must be submitted as a Change Request.
The request must include a description of the change and justification.

SECTION: Impact Analysis
The project manager and technical team evaluate the impact of the change.
Impact analysis includes:
- Schedule impact
- Cost impact
- Resource impact
- Technical risks

SECTION: Approval Process
After analysis, the change request must be approved by the project manager or change control board (CCB).
Only approved changes may be implemented.

SECTION: Scope Creep Prevention
Unauthorized changes should not be implemented.
All changes must follow the formal change management process.
